Out of the ordinary

"Out of the Ordinary" by Elisa Carabott transforms a traditional still life into a vibrant fusion of natural forms and digital artistry. With intricate details and vivid colours, it celebrates nature’s resilience and invites viewers to find wonder in the mundane, highlighting art and technology’s transformative power.
