Echoes of the Invisible

'"Echoes of the Invisible" is an immersive art installation by Stefan Mizzi that explores the fleeting nature of perception, memory, and time. Combining light, sound, and kinetic sculptures, the installation creates an ever-changing environment where visitors become part of the artwork. Through interactive elements that respond to motion, heat, and touch, Mizzi invites viewers to confront the intangible—those moments that slip through our grasp yet shape our understanding of reality. It’s a meditation on presence and absence, urging us to find meaning in the ephemeral.
