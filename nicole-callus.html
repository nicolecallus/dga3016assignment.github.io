Spectra

Spectra is a digital arts exhibition featuring a diverse range of artistic projects created using a vast variety of media. This exhibition represents the skills and knowledge which we have gained during these past three years while reading for a BFA in Digital Arts. Apart from portraying a specific theme in an art piece, certain art projects are used to as a means of educating the public. Spectra features the diverse subject matters that each artist has explored using their preferred artistic medium such as editorial design, graphic design, visual narrative, animation, game design, VR, AR, photography, installation art, mixed media and fashion design. Spectra is an opportunity to showcase each artists unique perspectives and artstic styles, showcasing the vast possibilities that digital art can offer.
