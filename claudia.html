Roots beneath the surface

"Roots Beneath the Surface" by Claudia Agius is an immersive installation exploring the hidden ties between nature, heritage, and humanity. Featuring glowing ceramic roots, ancestral wooden sculptures, and projected natural cycles, it reflects on the invisible networks sustaining life and identity. The work is a sensory meditation on the fragility and resilience of our connections.
